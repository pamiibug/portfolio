About

Self Kara is a Florida-based jewelry brand dedicated to boosting self-confidence and offering a stylish coping mechanism. Born from a teaching background, Self Kara was inspired by the need for young people to manage stress during difficult times. With a strong community focus, their aesthetic pieces aim to uplift and empower, blending beauty with purpose.

Strategy

Self Kara knew marketing would be the no. 1 thing to make or break a new business but did not know where to start. We crafted a plan that aligns seamlessly with Self Kara's identity, emphasizing a feminine and sophisticated aesthetic while engaging with current social trends. This approach is designed to resonate with millennial and Gen Z women, fostering a vibrant community and building lasting relationships with our target audience.
