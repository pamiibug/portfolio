Challenge

The brand needs to appeal to young cultured audiences while being fun and lively. The challenge was to take the brand's message and create a visual identity portraying it while targeting their niche listeners.

Solution

Inspired by the bold, funky personality of streetwear brands, I incorporated elements that would capture audience attention with a fresh, dynamic style. We began with the color blue to represent knowledge and reliability. Then, added textured typography and illustrations to connect with a younger audience.
Work in Progess
We word-for-word translated "Citizens of the World" into an illustration that perfectly describes the podcast.
Reflecting back to Black experiences, I chose to incorporate the Egyptian sun hieroglyph, a symbol of the passage of time, to connect with the podcast's exploration of lived experiences in
this lifetime.
To add, I created icons that connect to travel with a style that resembles hieroglyphs.
