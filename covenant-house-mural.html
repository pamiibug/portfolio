About
Partnering with Covenant House of California's Santa Clara branch, I designed and painted two walls of floor-to-ceiling murals to inspire youth. These vibrant, expansive murals are dedicated to showcasing themes of growth, life, and creativity, aiming to spark imagination and encourage a positive outlook.
