Mural Design
Large-scale murals to invoke social change
About
In partnership with Stories for Solidarity, I designed two 6x6 murals located in the heart of downtown San Jose, San Antonio Paseo and E Santa Clara St. These murals were created as a powerful response to the 2020 Black Lives Matter movement, a time when local businesses boarded up their shops in the face of uncertainty. The murals serve not only as a striking visual enhancement but also as a symbol of solidarity and support, blending beauty with a message of social justice.
